# Merlin — agent marketing ideas

_A working brainstorm for marketing the agents in Merlin. Companion to
[`merlin-overview.md`](./merlin-overview.md), [`website-content.md`](./website-content.md),
and the catalog in [`agents-library.md`](./agents-library.md). Framing matches the
docs: an **AI co-worker for buildings**, delivered by a **growing fleet of general
and specialized agents**, grounded in 8,000+ buildings and 5+ years of telemetry._

---

## Campaign concept: "Meet the team"

The throughline: **Merlin isn't one AI — it's a team of co-workers.** Every great
team has two kinds of people: **generalists who run the floor and coordinate**, and
**specialists who go deep on one thing**. Merlin's fleet is built exactly that way —
a few general agents that triage the whole building and route the work, and a
growing roster of specialists who each own a single concern. You'd never hire one
person to do plumbing, refrigeration, security, and compliance. Merlin gives you the
whole bench — already trained on 8,000+ buildings.

**Format ideas for the concept**

- **Roster / "Meet the team" page** — each agent as a colleague: icon, name, role,
  one-line "what they handle," and their signature moment. Split into _Generalists_
  and _Specialists_.
- **Trading cards** — name + specialty + "stats" (decisions/day, avg response time,
  SLAs watched, autonomy level). Collectible, shareable, demo-friendly.
- **Job-posting treatment** — each agent as the role it fills: _"Now hiring: Restroom
  Compliance Lead. Starts immediately. Never sleeps. Already trained on 8,000
  buildings."_
- **Before / With vignettes** — _"Without the Cold-Chain agent: a sensor blip and a
  six-figure loss. With it: caught at minute two."_
- **"The fleet keeps growing"** — a recurring beat: new specialists join the team
  over time, with no change to the hardware on your walls. Position it like a company
  that keeps hiring the best people.

## The generalists — general agents

The team leads. Broad, cross-cutting, always-on — they see the whole building, decide
what matters, and hand the deep work to the right specialist.

**🧭 The Concierge — _"Runs the floor so you don't have to."_**
The generalist that reads the entire building at once, triages everything happening,
and surfaces the single question that matters: _"What needs a decision in the next 30
minutes?"_ Routes each item to the specialist who owns it.
_Tagline: "One co-worker who sees the whole building — and knows who to call."_

**💬 Merlin (the assistant) — _"Ask it anything about your building."_**
The conversational generalist. Knows every space, sensor, route, and SLA, and the
work the whole fleet is doing. _"Why did energy spike last month?" "Which restrooms
are overdue?" "Draft the contractor a summary."_ — answered from live data, in plain
language.
_Tagline: "The one colleague who's read every report — and remembers all of them."_

**🌅 The Briefer — _"Your stand-up, already done."_**
Turns overnight into a one-screen morning brief: what changed, what's at risk, what's
already handled. You start the day informed, not catching up.
_Tagline: "Walk in knowing. Every morning."_

## The specialists — specialized agents

Each one is the best in the world at exactly one job.

**🧹 Cleaning agent — _"Never misses a spot."_**
Knows every restroom's status before anyone complains. Reads NFC taps, footfall, and
requests; sends the crew to the right place at the right time — and proves it was done.
_Hero moment: "Floor 9 restroom — 18h since last clean and a fresh request. Dispatch
now?" → tap → done, logged._

**🌡️ HVAC / Comfort agent — _"Comfort on autopilot."_**
Tells a seasonal drift from a dying compressor, and schedules the fix while it's still
cheap.
_Tagline: "Catches the breakdown while it's still just a flicker."_

**❄️ Cold-chain agent — _"Guards what can't get warm."_**
End-to-end temperature compliance that knows the rules — pharma vs. food vs. biologics.
_Tagline: "The difference between a sensor blip and a six-figure loss."_

**💊 Pharmacy temperature agent — _"Audit-grade, every degree."_**
Clinical refrigeration that's always inspection-ready, with traceability for every
reading.
_Tagline: "Compliance you can hand to a regulator without flinching."_

**🛡️ Security agent — _"Knows routine from wrong."_**
Watches every access pattern; raises the alarm only when it actually matters.
_Tagline: "Eyes on everything. Alerts on what counts."_

**🅿️ Parking agent — _"Ends the circling."_**
Live occupancy, violation detection, and availability pushed to the Smart Display at
the entrance.
_Tagline: "Tells drivers where to go before they loop the garage."_

**⚡ Energy / Savings agent — _"Finds the money leaking out the walls."_**
Tracks consumption against budget in real time; flags the zone running hot or the
system cycling through an empty building.
_Tagline: "Spots the leak before the bill does."_

**📋 Compliance agent — _"Pass the audit before it's scheduled."_**
Holds every space to its rules, regulations, and certifications; the evidence builds
itself.
_Tagline: "Continuous compliance, not an annual scramble."_
